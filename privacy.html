Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Huemood is a mood tracking app built by Maud. Your privacy matters, so this policy is simple: Huemood does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All data you enter in the app, including your mood logs and notes, is stored on your device and synced via iCloud so it transfers seamlessly to your new devices. Your data stays within your personal iCloud account and is never accessible to us. Huemood has no account system, no analytics, and no third-party integrations.

If you have any questions, you can reach out at hi@mcsquared.nl.
